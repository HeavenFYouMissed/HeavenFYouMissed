### 🔬 Model Surgery — My Current Research

> *Transplanting knowledge between neural networks. No retraining. Just math.*

Most people think if you want an AI to know something new, you have to retrain it. I found a way to copy-paste knowledge directly from one model into another using linear algebra on the weights.

| Scale | Models | Alignment |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| 124M | GPT-2 → DistilGPT-2 | **91.7%** |
| 7B | Pythia → Mistral (cross-arch) | **98.9%** |
| 70B | LLaMA-3.1 → Qwen2.5 | **99%+** |

The bigger the model, the better it works. Opposite of what you'd expect.
